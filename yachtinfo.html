Overview

Elegance meets luxury and performance with Zest For Life, our 68 Azimut. Expansive seating throughout the interiors and exteriors will have you sailing in comfort. Boasting four staterooms with en suite bathrooms, the Azimut does not fail to deliver a wonderful day or multi day trip to one of our beautiful destinations. Unique to vessels in the size range, Zest For Life has an enlarged bow seating area. If you are into music, we have great news with our newly upgraded the sound system found throughout the yacht. Our fun, yet professional crew will make sure you have a wonderful trip. We look forward to welcoming you aboard soon for an unforgettable day on the water!
