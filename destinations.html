Destinations

We strive to give each guest a perfect trip to one of our coveted Destinations in Miami, The Florida Keys, or The Bahamas. Find each option below!

Miami

On our Miami itinerary (most popular!), guests can expect to enjoy sights such as the Downtown Miami skyline, Fisher Island, the Flagler Monument, Star Island, the Key Biscayne Marine Stadium and sandbar. We also cruise through Brickell down the Miami River where you can find dining options like Seaspice and Kiki's On The River. If time permits, we can even stop for lunch or dinner at one of these oceanfront restaurants. Alternatively, you may request a chef or catering be provided on board!

Depending on the time of day you reserve, we may cater your trip to make sure you able to enjoy the beautiful and warm sun and the magical sunset.

The Florida Keys (multi-day only)

In the Florida Keys, enjoy the comforts of all 4 staterooms we have to offer! Key Largo, Islamorada, and Key West are a perfect trip to make sure you get the full experience. Activities include sightseeing and snorkeling through the beautiful underwater scenery that the Keys have to offer.

The Bahamas (multi-day only)

In the Bahamas, guests can also enjoy the 4 wonderful staterooms we have to offer! Trips to the Bahamas vary by trip length but our most popular destination is Bimini. Bimini, just 50 miles from Miami, has the famous turquoise water found in the Bahamas combined with the amenities of a resort and fantastic shipwrecks to explore.
