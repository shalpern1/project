Project Proposal

Over the last year, I have been working on building Boating For Pleasure, my boat management company in Miami, Florida. Boating For Pleasure currently manages one boat which is rented out by tourists and locals. The goal of my term project will be to create a website for my company. The website should have the following:

  • Pictures of the boat
  • Boat information
  • Calendar showing available dates
  • Pricing
  • Sightseeing options
  • Contact information
  • Customer Reviews
